Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings > "Restore Purchases" or tap the Restore button on the paywall screen.

Does LockedIn sync across devices?

Yes. If you are signed in, your focus history and stats sync across all your iOS devices.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account and all associated data directly within the app under Settings > Data > Delete Account.
