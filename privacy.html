Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Your privacy is important to us. It is LockedIn's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account, we may collect your email address and basic profile information to authenticate you and sync your data across devices.

Usage Data

We collect data on how you use the app, specifically your focus sessions (duration, timestamps, labels) to provide you with statistics and insights. This data is stored securely on our servers.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the app.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our app's functionality.
  • Analyze usage patterns to help you build better habits.

3. Data Storage and Security

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

4. Third-Party Services

We may employ third-party companies, such as RevenueCat for subscription management and Supabase for backend services, due to the following reasons:

  • To facilitate our Service;
  • To provide the Service on our behalf;

These third parties have access to your Personal Information only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you. You also have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time via the "Delete Account" option in the app settings.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@lockedin.app.
